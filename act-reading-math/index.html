ACT Reading & Math Trainer

A focused set of tools to help students practice ACT-style reading passages, timing, and math skills.

Planned features

  • Reading passages modeled after ACT difficulty and length, with adjustable timing.
  • Math practice on high-yield topics such as algebra, functions, and geometry.
  • Short, repeatable sessions so students can build stamina and pacing over time.
  • Clear feedback to highlight which question types and topics need more work.
  • No ads and no accounts – a distraction-free environment focused purely on practice.

This app is under active development. The first release will focus on reading and math modules, with additional English and science content planned for future versions.

Privacy & Data

The ACT Reading & Math Trainer will follow the same privacy approach as our other apps: no student accounts, no third-party advertising, and no sale of personal data. For full details, please see the Neuron Learning Labs Privacy Policy.
