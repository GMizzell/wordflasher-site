Christmas Word Search

A festive 29×29 word search puzzle built around classic Christmas carols, with both Classic and Challenge modes.

What the app does

  • Large 29×29 puzzle grid packed with Christmas carol titles hidden in all directions.
  • Classic mode – choose any song from the list, search at your own pace, and tap letters to trace the title in the grid.
  • Challenge mode – work against a one-, two-, or three-minute timer for each song title. Miss it and it goes back into the clue pool.
  • Tap-to-trace – when you find a song, tap each letter in order; correct paths stay lightly highlighted so you can see your progress.
  • No ads, no accounts – the app does not require a login and does not include advertising.

Who it’s for

Christmas Word Search is designed for families, puzzle fans, and anyone who enjoys Christmas music and wants a cozy brain exercise during the holiday season. It’s also a fun way to practice attention and pattern recognition.

Privacy & Data

Christmas Word Search does not collect personally identifiable information. The app does not require user accounts or registrations. Any anonymized usage analytics are handled by Apple through standard App Store tools. For full details, please see the Neuron Learning Labs Privacy Policy.
