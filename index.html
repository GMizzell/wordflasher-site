Word Flasher

Turn your phone into a bold, full‑screen message board. Type two words, pick colors, set the speed, and press Start.

Support • Privacy

What it does

  • Big, auto‑sized text that fills the screen
  • Per‑word color pickers (text & background)
  • Speed slider (0.25–5.00s)
  • Tap to pause/resume; one tap to close
  • No account. No ads. No data collected.

How to use

  1. Type two words (e.g., GO and TEAM).
  2. Pick colors for text and background.
  3. Adjust the speed, then tap Start.
