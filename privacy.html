Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Neuron Learning Labs (“we,” “our,” “us”) develops educational and puzzle-based apps including:

  • Christmas Song Word Search
  • WordFlasher Reading Helper
  • ACT Reading & Math Trainer

This Privacy Policy explains how our apps handle your data. In short: our apps do not collect, store, track, or transmit any personal data whatsoever.

1. Data We Do Not Collect

Our apps do not collect any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Contacts or photos
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or app activity
  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA)
  • Diagnostic or crash data (except what Apple provides anonymously)

2. No Accounts or Login

Our apps do not require any form of login or account setup. All features are fully available without creating an account.

3. No Internet Tracking

Our apps do not track your behavior, do not connect to external servers for analytics, and do not use cookies or third-party tracking technologies.

4. Local-Only Data

Any app progress—such as completed word search items or reading timers—is stored locally on your device only. We never access or transmit it.

5. Children’s Privacy

Our apps are safe for children and comply with COPPA because no data is collected from users of any age.
