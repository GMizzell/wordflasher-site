Reading Helper

A focused app to build reading speed and accuracy for students.

Reading Helper is a training app designed to help students improve real-world reading speed and accuracy, especially for tests like the ACT and SAT.

The app is built around short, focused exercises that train eye quickness, attention, and comprehension without distractions.

Flash Trainer

The Flash Trainer briefly shows words and phrases on the screen, then asks the student to type exactly what they saw.

  • Levels from 4-letter words up to longer 20–30+ character phrases
  • Adjustable flash duration and accuracy tracking
  • Automatic level progression as students improve
  • Includes both familiar phrases and “non-intuitive” combinations, so the brain cannot simply guess from context

Reading Speed Practice (coming soon)

The upcoming Reading Speed Practice module will include full passages grouped by reading level, with timing and comprehension questions.

  • Passages for grades 4–6, 6–8, 9–10, and 11–12
  • Words-per-minute (WPM) calculation for each session
  • Detail questions (what did the passage say?)
  • Inference questions (why did this happen? what can we conclude?)

Who is Reading Helper for?

  • Middle and high school students preparing for ACT and SAT
  • Students who read accurately but slowly and want to build speed
  • Adults returning to school who need to sharpen reading skills
