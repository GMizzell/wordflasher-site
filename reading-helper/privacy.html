Reading Helper – Privacy Policy

Last updated:

1. No account or registration

Reading Helper does not require you to create an account or sign in. The app does not ask for your name, email address, or other personal contact information.

2. Data stored on your device

Training progress, statistics, and settings are stored only on your device. This information is not uploaded to our servers.

3. No third-party advertising or tracking

Reading Helper does not include third-party advertising SDKs and does not track you across other apps or websites.

4. Crash reports and diagnostics

If you have enabled sharing crash reports and diagnostics with Apple, Apple may provide us with anonymous, aggregated information through its developer tools. This information does not identify you personally and is used only to fix bugs and improve app stability.

5. Children and students

Reading Helper is appropriate for students and may be used by children under the guidance of a parent, teacher, or tutor. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how Reading Helper works, please contact:
george@allpencilsdown.com
